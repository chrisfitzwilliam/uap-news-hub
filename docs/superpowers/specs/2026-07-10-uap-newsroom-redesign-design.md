# Skyledger Redesign Design Specification
**Date:** 2026-07-10
**Domain:** skyledger.site

## 1. Brand & Tone
- **Name:** Skyledger
- **Tone:** Authoritative, neutral, scientific, and data-driven.
- **Audience:** Balanced between curious mainstream readers and serious UAP researchers, leaning slightly toward serious research.

## 2. Visual Direction: "Night Sky Observatory"
- **Color Palette:** 
  - Backgrounds: Near-black/deep space tones.
  - Text: Lunar white for high readability.
  - Accents: Restrained electric cyan (used sparingly for data plots, interactive highlights, and call-to-actions).
- **Typography:** Spacious, premium sans-serif typography (e.g., Inter, Roboto, or Outfit) for a clean, modern aesthetic.
- **Styling Rules:** No cartoon-alien tropes or generic "purple AI" styling. Emphasis on scientific plotting, clean lines, and data visualization elements.

## 3. Layout & Hierarchy
- **Homepage Structure:** "Briefing + Evidence Grid"
- **Top Section (The Lead Briefing):** An accessible, immediate summary of the current state of UAP intelligence and news.
- **Middle Section (The Evidence Grid):** A structured, easily navigable dashboard containing:
  - Evidence library
  - Claim tracker
  - YouTube intelligence/transcripts
  - Source directories
  - Methodology explanation

## 4. Underlying Architecture
- The visual redesign applies exclusively to the front-end presentation.
- The existing evidence-first, local-first, deterministic, fail-closed publishing architecture remains untouched.
