// The method

Every claim carries its evidence.

Observation, interpretation, and speculation are kept separate. A report can be newsworthy without proving a cause.

01

Source-linked

Every briefing links to the primary NASA, AARO, ODNI, or congressional document behind it.

02

Confidence-graded

Each item is tagged high, medium, or low based on the strength of the underlying record.

03

Unresolved ≠ confirmed

An unresolved case stays labeled unresolved until stronger evidence changes the assessment.

The confidence ladder
High

Primary documents, corroborated or officially stated.

Medium

Analytic reading of a source; some open questions.

Low

Single-source or transcript-derived; held for review.

// The pipeline

From raw source to verified record

1 Ingest

RSS news feeds and 10 monitored YouTube channels.

2 Triage

Source scoring filters noise from candidate material.

3 Transcribe

Local Whisper transcription of downloaded audio.

4 Analyze

AGY extracts timestamped, source-grounded claims.

5 Review

Factual gate; fails closed on any schema or budget error.

6 Publish

Only validated JSON is committed to the public record.
